Ingredients

  • Premium cocoa
  • Pure butter
  • Condensed milk
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare Pan: Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper. Let some overhang for easy lifting later.
  2. Wet Ingredients: In a saucepan, combine sugar, evaporated milk, butter, and marshmallow creme (if using). Heat over medium heat, stirring constantly, until boiling.
  3. Boil and Stir: Boil the mixture for 5 minutes, stirring constantly. This ensures everything dissolves and reaches the right consistency.
  4. Remove from Heat: Take the pan off the heat.
  5. Add Chocolate: Add chocolate chips (milk, semi-sweet, or a combination) and stir until melted and smooth.
  6. Flavoring (Optional): Stir in vanilla extract and any other desired flavorings (like peppermint or caramel extract) at this point.
  7. Pour and Set: Pour the fudge mixture into the prepared pan. Spread evenly with a spatula.
  8. Cooling: Let the fudge cool completely at room temperature, for at least 2 hours.
  9. Refrigerate (Optional): You can refrigerate the fudge for a firmer texture, about 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  10. Cutting and Serving: Once set, use the parchment paper overhang to lift the fudge out of the pan. Cut into squares and enjoy your creamy chocolate fudge!
